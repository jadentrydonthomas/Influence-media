# Your media goes here

Drop your real photos and video thumbnails into this `assets/` folder, then they can
be shown on the site. Suggested files:

- `post1.jpg` … `post4.jpg` — screenshots/thumbnails of your top posts (the 2.7M video, etc.)
- `logo.png` or `logo.svg` — your real Influence Media logo (optional; the site currently
  uses a clean re-creation built in code)

## How the post thumbnails get used
In `index.html`, the "Results" section has four `.post-card` boxes. To show a real image
in one, add a background image and the `has-img` class, for example:

```html
<div class="post-card has-img reveal" style="background-image:url('assets/post1.jpg')">
  <span class="post-card__handle">@sapodillasdc</span>
  <span class="post-card__play">▶</span>
  <div class="post-card__stats"><span>▶ 2.7M</span><span>♥ 49K</span></div>
</div>
```

## Easiest option
Just send the image/video files to me in chat and tell me which post each one is —
I'll place them, set the right view counts, and push the update for you.

## Want your live Instagram posts embedded instead?
Send me the **links** to the specific posts (open a post on instagram.com and copy the URL,
e.g. `https://www.instagram.com/p/ABC123/`). I'll embed them so they render live and stay
up to date automatically.
