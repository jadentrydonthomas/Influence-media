Phase 1 · Start Here

Automate the Engine

Pinterest drives the traffic · Amazon closes the sale · You collect while you sleep

→
Why Pinterest?
  • 70% of Pinterest users are actively shopping or planning to buy — it's a search engine for people ready to spend
  • The "luxury pink aesthetic" is one of the biggest performing niches on Pinterest right now
  • Pins live forever — unlike TikTok or Instagram posts that die in 24 hours, a single pin can drive traffic for years
  • Amazon Associates pays 1–10% commission on everything a visitor buys within 24 hours — not just your product, anything in their cart
Set Up Your Pinterest for Sales
1
Optimize Your Profile
Use keywords in your display name AND bio. Try: "Luxury Pink Aesthetic · Amazon Finds · Home Decor." Pinterest is a search engine — keywords are how people find you.
2
Create 10–15 Niche Boards
Name them exactly how people search. "Pink Bedroom Decor Ideas", "Luxury Home Aesthetic", "Amazon Pink Finds Under 0." Specific beats generic every time.
3
Enable Rich Pins
Rich Pins automatically pull product price and availability from Amazon. They look more professional, get higher click rates, and take about 10 minutes to set up. Free.
4
Pin Size Matters — Always 2:3 Ratio
Use 1000×1500px. Pinterest's algorithm buries non-vertical pins. Canva has dozens of free Pinterest templates — don't design from scratch.
5
Add Text Overlays to Your Pins
"Found on Amazon · Pink Velvet Chair 9" performs 3× better than just the image alone. Tell people what it is and what it costs right on the pin.
Daily Pinterest Strategy
  • Pin 15–25 times per day — 80% repins from others in your niche, 20% your original content
  • Best posting times: 8–11pm weeknights and all day Saturday (when people browse and dream)
  • Use Tailwind to schedule pins in advance — set it once a week and forget it
  • Join group boards — search "pink aesthetic group boards", request to join the active ones. Instant amplification.
  • Video pins get 3× more reach than static images — even a simple slideshow in Canva qualifies
Amazon Associates Setup
  • Sign up free at affiliate-program.amazon.com — you get a unique link for every product
  • When someone clicks your link and buys anything within 24 hours — you earn. Not just the item you linked.
  • Focus on products in the 0–50 range — sweet spot between commission size and buyer willingness
  • Best categories: home decor, bedroom accessories, aesthetic pink items, kitchen gadgets, organization
  • Build a curated storefront — "Luxury Pink Picks" collections make it easy to send followers to a page full of your favorites
Milestone — When Are You Ready for Phase 2?
  • Pinterest showing 10,000+ monthly views
  • Consistent, trackable clicks through to your Amazon storefront
  • Earning at least 00–00/month passively from affiliate commissions
  • You can feel the momentum — people are saving your pins, boards are growing. That's when you layer in Phase 2.

Phase 2 · Brand Building

Show Your Face. Build Your Name.

TikTok · TikTok Shop · Instagram · Multiple income streams, one audience

+
Why Content Now?
  • Your Pinterest already proved what your audience responds to — now you put a face to it and build trust
  • "Amazon Finds" is one of the biggest TikTok niches ever. Videos in this space hit millions of views regularly.
  • People buy from people they like. Your face = trust = higher conversions than any anonymous pin
  • You're not starting from zero — Phase 1 already showed you which products and aesthetics your audience loves
TikTok Strategy
1
The Hook Is Everything
Your first 3 seconds decide if people stay. Start with "I found the most aesthetic Amazon find" or "POV: your room could look like this for 0." Hook first, everything else second.
2
Post 1–2× Daily
TikTok rewards consistency more than perfection. Done is better than perfect. Film in batches — film 7 videos in one session, post one per day. You don't need a studio, just good light.
3
Use Trending Sounds Strategically
Find sounds with 20K–200K uses (not millions — less competition, still trending). The sweet spot gets you visibility without being buried under 10,000 other creators.
4
Best Content Formats
Unboxings, "Amazon finds under ", room transformations, hauls, "things that make your space look expensive." These are proven formulas — don't reinvent, just execute with your aesthetic.
5
Always End With a CTA
"Link in bio for everything I showed" or "Comment PINK and I'll send you the link." Comments boost your video in the algorithm — you want people engaging, not just watching.
TikTok Shop — Extra Money From the Same Videos
  • TikTok Shop lets viewers buy directly inside the app — no leaving TikTok, no friction, higher conversion
  • You earn 5–20% commission on every sale through your Shop videos
  • Add product links to your existing videos — they show up as a shopping bag icon on screen
  • You need 1,000 followers to apply. Reach that first, then activate Shop immediately — same videos, extra income stream
  • Best approach: link the exact products you show in each video so the connection is seamless for the viewer
Instagram Strategy
  • Reels: Repost your best TikToks (remove the TikTok watermark first — use SnapTik, it's free)
  • Stories: Behind the scenes, polls ("which one should I get?"), product reveals — this builds real relationship with followers
  • Instagram Shopping: Connect your Amazon store link, add product tags directly to your posts and Reels
  • Bio link: Use Beacons.ai or Linktree — one link that routes to everything: Amazon store, TikTok, Pinterest
  • Post Reels 4–5×/week, Stories daily. Instagram loves consistency.
Finding Your Winners
  • Post 10–15 different products. See which 3 get the most comments, saves, and shares — those are your winners
  • Double down on winners — make more content about those products and similar items. Don't spread thin.
  • Watch your comments obsessively: what are people asking for? What are they excited about? That's your market speaking directly to you.
  • Screenshot your best-performing posts — these become the blueprint for everything you make going forward
  • A product that gets 50 comments asking "where do I get this?" is a product that could be your own brand someday
Milestone — When Are You Ready for Phase 3?
  • 10,000+ followers on TikTok or Instagram (or combined)
  • People tagging you in their posts — "got this because of her!"
  • Comments like "do you have your own brand?" or "I'd buy anything you made"
  • You've made ,000+ in a single month from content
  • That's when you stop selling other people's products and start selling your own.

Phase 3 · The Final Play

Your Own Product. Your Empire.

The audience is built · The trust is earned · Now you own everything

✦ ♛ ✦
You're Ready When...
  • People are coming to YOU specifically — not just Pinterest or TikTok in general. You have a name.
  • You've identified 2–3 product types your audience goes crazy for — you've seen it in the comments
  • You've proven demand: your Amazon affiliate links for those products convert consistently
  • You have an audience that trusts your taste. That is the most valuable thing in business — and you've built it.
Finding YOUR Winning Product
1
Look at Your Data
Which Amazon products did you drive the most clicks and sales to? That category is your lane. The data is telling you what to make — listen to it.
2
Read the Amazon Reviews — Especially 1 and 2 Stars
"I wish this came in pink." "The quality isn't luxury enough." "The packaging felt cheap." Every complaint is a product opportunity. That's YOUR product.
3
Your Own Gap
Is there something YOU wanted but couldn't find? If you want it — a luxury-feel item at a fair price in that pink aesthetic — your audience probably wants it too.
4
Keep It Simple to Start
One great product, done right, beats a whole catalog done badly. Nail the first one — the quality, the packaging, the experience — then expand from there.
How to Create Your Product — 3 Paths
Path A · Fastest (4–8 weeks)
Private Label
  • Find a product on Alibaba that's already selling well — search your niche
  • Request samples from 3–5 suppliers. Pick the best quality.
  • Add your logo, choose your colors (pink, luxury gold). Make it yours.
  • Minimum orders typically start at 50–200 units — very low to start
  • The math: Buy at , sell at 5. That's 70%+ profit margin.
Path B · Zero Cost (Start Tomorrow)
Digital Products
  • Aesthetic room guides, Pinterest strategy courses, mood board PDFs, Lightroom preset packs
  • Create it once, sell it forever — zero inventory, zero shipping
  • Sell through Gumroad (free) or your own Shopify store (9/month)
  • Your audience already trusts your taste — they will pay for your knowledge and your eye
  • A 7 "Luxury Pink Room Makeover Guide" to 10,000 followers is a real income stream
Path C · Highest Margin (3–6 months)
Custom Design
  • Work with a manufacturer on something unique — designed by you, for your audience
  • Higher upfront cost but the product is fully yours and can't be easily copied
  • Best for when you have a clear audience and a very specific gap to fill
  • This is the play that turns a content creator into a brand owner
Launch Strategy
1
Tease It First
"I'm working on something for you guys…" — a few posts before launch builds anticipation. People will be watching for it.
2
Pre-Orders Validate AND Fund You
Post "pre-order now and save 20%" before you've manufactured. This confirms demand AND your customers fund your first order. Zero financial risk.
3
The Launch Video
"I finally made this because of you all" — show the product, tell the story behind it. Why did you make it? What problem does it solve? Emotion sells.
4
Limited First Drop
"Only 100 available" creates real urgency. Sell out fast, restock, repeat. Selling out is a marketing event — it proves demand and creates FOMO for the next drop.
5
Leverage Every Platform at Once
Pin it. TikTok it. Story it. All on the same day. The audience you built across Phase 1 and Phase 2 is about to make your launch massive.
