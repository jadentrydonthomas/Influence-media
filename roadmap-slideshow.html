Phase 1 · Right Now
Automate the Engine
Pinterest drives the traffic · Amazon closes the sale · You collect
Pinterest
→
Amazon
1
Set up your luxury Pinterest aesthetic
Pink, elevated, aspirational. Your pins should stop the scroll — think high-end lifestyle, curated products, dreamy visuals.
2
Automate daily pinning
Use a scheduler (Tailwind or later) to post 5–15 pins per day consistently. The algorithm rewards volume + consistency.
3
Every pin links to your Amazon storefront
Pinterest traffic flows directly to Amazon. Amazon handles the sale, shipping, and payment. You earn commissions passively.
Goal: Build stable monthly income before moving to Phase 2
Phase 2 · Brand Building
Show Your Face.
Build Your Name.
TikTok · TikTok Shop · Instagram · Multiple revenue streams, one audience
TikTok
+
Instagram
1
Start creating short-form content
Unboxings, lifestyle clips, product showcases. Keep it real, keep it you. TikTok & Reels reward consistency — post daily and watch the algorithm work.
2
Use TikTok Shop + link-in-bio to monetize every view
TikTok Shop lets people buy directly inside the app. Instagram bio links to your Amazon store. Every platform becomes a checkout lane — multiple ways to earn from the same content.
3
Build the brand — you are the product
People buy into YOU first, the product second. Every follower gained here is an asset. The bigger the audience, the louder the launch when Phase 3 comes. This is where the real leverage gets built.
Goal: Build a loyal following that trusts your recommendation
Phase 3 · The Final Play
Your Own Product.
Your Empire.

Your Brand
1
You have a platform — people come to you
By this point your audience shows up because of YOU. They're loyal, they're engaged, and they're ready to buy what you create.
2

3

The Pinterest + Amazon machine that started it all now drives traffic to YOUR store. No middleman. No split. Maximum profit.
